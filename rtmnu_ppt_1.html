Profile Information Required

  • Full name, date of birth, gender, category
  • Domicile & nationality details
  • Aadhaar number (securely masked)
  • ABC ID — Academic Bank of Credits
  • Passport-size photograph upload
  • Signature image upload
  • Government photo ID proof
DigiLocker Integration: Documents are fetched directly from DigiLocker — no physical copies needed at the time of application. Reduces fraud and speeds up verification significantly.
